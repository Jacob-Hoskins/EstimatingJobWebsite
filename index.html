IET

Your Automated Estimating Tool

Download Here

IET is a automated estimating tool designed for construction companies. If you're a home remodeling business and you want to give accurate estimates without doing the math this is the tool for you. If you need drywall, screws, 2x4's whatever it may be, this tool can get you the price of everything in minutes. If not seconds depending on the amount of materials needed. Not only that but you can add quantity to each item you'll need for the job and save the output and print it for the client. Each item will tell you how much it cost for one unit, the total price for the total of units you need. And the final price of everything.

Depending on the amount of material you'll need for the job, IET can get your estiamte done in less than 10 minutes!

It's FREE! One day in the future there's a chance this could cost but for the time being it's free to download. All because we want to wait till the app is perfect and just needs minor updates.
