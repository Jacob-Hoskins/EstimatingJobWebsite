Your Estimating Tech Tool

Doing estimates can take a long time, between talking to the customer, figuring out everything you`ll need, getting the prices of everything, doing the math, and putting it on an organized document for your client to see. Lucky for we can do 90% of that for you in a few minutes.

What is this?

This is a automated itemized estimating tool made for home remodeling companies, by home remodeling workers

But prices always change!

Don`t worry, we thought about that! When you start your search the prices you get are the real time prices. We also make sure to put a disclaimer on the print out of your itemized sheet to let the client know prices can change or vary from location to location.

What exactly does it do?

This tool gives you accurate itemized estimates all on its own. The only thing you have to do is let it know what materials you`ll need to get the clients job done

Where do these prices come from?

Currently we go through Home Depot to get prices for your itemized estimates. In the future we hope to be able to use Lowes, Home Depot, and other popular stores to get your itemized estimates.
